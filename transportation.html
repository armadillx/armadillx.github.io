Transportation

Getting where you're going

If you don't have a car or easy access to public transportation, there are options for getting to the resources you need.

The Houston METRO offers a trip planner so you can find a bus, METRORail, or bike route to your destination.

The Montrose Center's volunteer program has a pool of verified community members and allies who can give rides to anyone in need. You can sign up to get a ride and you'll be matched with someone who's available to drive you to:

  • The Montrose Center
  • The resource you need to access
  • The nearest bus stop or Park and Ride to you

If you have a car and you regularly go to events at the Montrose Center or other LGBTQIA+ organizations, consider signing up as a volunteer with the Montrose Center! Then you can help out by giving rides to others who need that resource.
